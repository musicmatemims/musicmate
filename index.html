LinkedIn for independent musicians

MusicMate is a mobile web application made by music lovers for music lovers. Think LinkedIn for independent musicians. MusicMate offers its users a place to shine, exchange ideas, and find gigs to take their music career to the next level

About MusicMate

A platform dedicated to help musicians expand their network and find gig opportunities

We are building a platform that connects musicians and music lovers from around the world.

Our goal is to build a thriving community of musicians who can collaborate, learn from each other, and produce music that makes people happy!

MusicMate offers a user-friendly platform that caters to the demands of the music community, with tools that help musicians locate gigs and chances to exhibit their talents in public spaces nearby.

Core Features

We have four core modules

Onboarding and Profile Creation

User's choose what aspects of their profile they want to showcase to others and what they want to be private.

Home Feed and Discovery

Our home feed and search functionality allows you to easily and quickly find relevant people, gigs and posts that might be of interest to you.

Build your network

Bookmark interesting profiles, find new band mates by viewing their portfolio or message other users.

Findings gigs and opportunities

Use our gig posting service to either find a job that interests you or post an opening to find just the right candidate.
